Delete Your Account

eventise lets users request deletion of their account and associated personal data. Use the steps below to request deletion.

How to request deletion

Send an account deletion request from the same email address used in your eventise account to:

dinesh.reachme@gmail.com

Request account deletion

What will be deleted

  • Your eventise account details linked to your login email.
  • Your profile information such as name, email address, and phone number stored in the app.
  • Your event data you created or manage, including guests, gifts, rooms, and related records, unless retention is required for active collaboration with other users.

What may be retained

  • Records required for fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute handling, or security purposes.
  • Backup data for a limited period before secure deletion from backup systems.

Retention period

We aim to process deletion requests within 7 business days. Backup or security logs may remain for up to 30 days before final deletion, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

If you signed in with Google, deleting your eventise account does not delete your Google account. This page is provided for Google Play account deletion compliance and user transparency.
